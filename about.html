Behind the Lens

I'm Karloz — a SAG actor turned photographer based in Los Angeles. That actor's perspective is my secret weapon behind the camera.

I know what it feels like to sit in front of a lens. The vulnerability, the pressure to perform, the hope that the camera catches something real. That understanding shapes every session I shoot. My job isn't just to take photos — it's to create the conditions where you can show up authentically.

Based in Los Angeles with a private home studio, I specialize in actor headshots, corporate portraits, and creative sessions for individuals who want images that genuinely represent them. Natural light and cinematic studio lighting. Relaxed direction. Fast, clean edits.

Whether you're submitting to casting directors, updating your LinkedIn, or building your personal brand — let's make something powerful together.
